# Intake Questionnaire

Use this reference before recommending platforms or content strategy. The goal is to understand the business well enough to connect platform choices to business needs.

## Required Context

Collect or infer the following:

- Company: what the company does, country or region, current China presence.
- Business type: B2B, B2C, marketplace, enterprise, education, community, developer, or mixed model.
- Target customer: decision maker, user, buyer, influencer, community, or channel partner.
- Product or service: software, hardware, AI, education, manufacturing, consumer goods, services, or other category.
- Primary objective: brand awareness, lead generation, sales, community building, customer education, customer support, search visibility, or partner development.
- Product traits: requires explanation, demonstration, trust building, long-form content, visual proof, lifestyle positioning, search discovery, or community interaction.
- Existing assets: website, blog, LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, case studies, white papers, tutorials, short videos, webinars, customer stories.
- Resources: Chinese-speaking team, marketing team, sales support, design/video capability, community manager, founder time, budget, and expected launch timing.

## Follow-Up Question Guidance

Ask concise questions only when needed. Prioritize:

1. What does the company sell, and to whom?
2. What is the main China objective for the next 3-6 months?
3. What existing content and team resources are available?

If the user does not know the answer, proceed with assumptions and label them clearly.

## Analysis Output

Summarize:

- Business type
- Target audience
- Product characteristics
- Marketing objectives
- Available resources
- Constraints or unknowns

Only continue to platform selection after this summary is clear.
