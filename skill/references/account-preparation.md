# Account Preparation

Use this reference to assess readiness for launching official or semi-official social accounts. Keep guidance practical and strategic. Do not claim definitive current platform registration, verification, advertising, or compliance rules.

## Readiness Areas

### Brand Assets

Prepare:

- Chinese brand name or approved transliteration
- Chinese product description
- One-line positioning statement
- Logo and avatar
- Banner or cover images
- Boilerplate company introduction
- Product screenshots or demo visuals
- Contact path or lead capture flow

### Account Positioning

Define for each platform:

- Account role
- Target audience
- Content promise
- Tone of voice
- Publishing cadence
- Community response owner
- Conversion path

### Operating Roles

Identify who owns:

- Strategy
- Chinese copywriting
- Design or video editing
- Publishing
- Comment moderation
- Community management
- Lead handoff
- Performance review

### Launch Content Inventory

Before launch, prepare enough content to avoid an empty account:

- 3-5 evergreen posts for each primary platform
- 1 pinned or introductory post
- 1 brand or product explainer
- 1 proof asset such as case study, testimonial, demo, or FAQ
- 1 conversion or contact post

### Account Verification and Compliance

Advise the user to verify current requirements through official platform documentation or qualified local support when they need:

- Official business verification
- Paid advertising
- Ecommerce functions
- Lead forms
- Mini programs
- Regulated industry claims
- Data collection or customer support workflows

## Output

Provide:

- Account readiness checklist
- Missing assets
- Recommended launch sequence
- Operating risks
- Items requiring current platform or legal verification
