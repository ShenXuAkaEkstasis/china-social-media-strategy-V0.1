# GTM Roadmap

Use this reference to create a phased China social media launch plan. Adjust timing based on resources and urgency.

## 0-30 Days: Foundation

Goals:

- Confirm China audience and priority use cases.
- Select primary and secondary platforms.
- Prepare Chinese positioning and account assets.
- Audit existing overseas content for reuse, adaptation, or recreation.
- Create initial content calendar.

Typical actions:

- Complete business and audience intake.
- Build platform recommendation matrix.
- Define content pillars.
- Prepare Chinese brand/profile assets.
- Adapt 3-5 launch posts for each primary platform.
- Identify account setup or verification dependencies.

Success indicators:

- Clear platform roles
- Launch-ready account profiles
- Initial content backlog
- Confirmed operating owner and cadence

## 31-60 Days: Launch and Learn

Goals:

- Launch primary platforms.
- Publish consistently.
- Test content formats and messaging.
- Start collecting engagement and search signals.

Typical actions:

- Publish educational, brand, and search-oriented content.
- Repurpose one anchor asset across selected platforms.
- Respond to comments and early community signals.
- Track simple metrics by platform.
- Identify top-performing topics and objections.

Success indicators:

- Consistent publishing rhythm
- Early engagement or search visibility
- Clearer audience questions
- First leads, followers, saves, comments, inquiries, or qualified conversations

## 61-90 Days: Optimize and Expand

Goals:

- Double down on effective channels and formats.
- Improve localization.
- Expand into secondary or vertical communities if justified.
- Connect content activity to business outcomes.

Typical actions:

- Refine content pillars based on performance.
- Create China-native content around local objections and use cases.
- Test webinars, community groups, demos, founder posts, or short video series.
- Build lead handoff or private traffic workflows where relevant.
- Evaluate whether to add paid campaigns or partnerships.

Success indicators:

- Better-performing content themes
- Stronger localized messaging
- Repeatable content workflow
- Clear next-quarter platform priorities

## Roadmap Output

Present a phased plan with:

- Phase goals
- Key actions
- Content priorities
- Platform focus
- Owner or capability needed
- Success indicators
- Risks and dependencies
