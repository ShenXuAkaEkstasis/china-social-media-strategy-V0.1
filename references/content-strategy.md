# Content Strategy

Recommend content strategy after business analysis and platform selection. Content should map to product complexity, customer awareness, purchase process, objectives, platform behavior, and available resources.

## Strategy Types

### Educational Content

Use when:

- The product is difficult to understand.
- Users need demonstrations or tutorials.
- Customer education is required before purchase.
- The buying process is complex.

Typical formats:

- Tutorials
- Product walkthroughs
- Feature demonstrations
- FAQs
- Case studies
- Comparison articles
- Webinars or replay clips

### Brand Content

Use when:

- Brand awareness is a priority.
- Emotional connection influences purchase.
- Lifestyle positioning matters.
- Visual or community identity is important.

Typical formats:

- Brand stories
- User stories
- Lifestyle posts
- Founder or team stories
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Community interaction

### Search-Oriented Content

Use when:

- Users actively search for solutions.
- Long-term inbound traffic matters.
- Professional credibility matters.
- The product category requires research before purchase.

Typical formats:

- Articles
- Guides
- Industry analysis
- Q&A answers
- Comparison posts
- Knowledge sharing

### Entertainment or Attention Content

Use when:

- Viral exposure is important.
- Short-form video fits the product.
- The brand can participate naturally in trends.
- Community engagement is a primary goal.

Typical formats:

- Short videos
- Trend participation
- Interactive posts
- Memes only when brand-appropriate
- Lightweight demos or before/after content

## Publishing Cadence

Adapt cadence to resources. Prefer consistency over aggressive volume.

- Low resource: 1-2 primary platforms, 2-4 posts per month per platform, repurposed assets.
- Moderate resource: 2-3 platforms, weekly publishing, one localized anchor asset per month.
- High resource: platform-specific content streams, weekly or multi-weekly publishing, active community management and performance iteration.

## Platform Adaptation

Do not publish the same content identically everywhere. Adapt:

- Hook and title
- Length and format
- Tone
- Examples
- Visual style
- Call to action
- Comment/community behavior

## Output

Recommend:

- Primary content strategy
- Secondary content strategy
- Content pillars
- Platform-specific formats
- Publishing cadence
- Reuse/adaptation/recreation priorities
- Early performance indicators
