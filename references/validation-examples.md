# Validation Examples

Use these examples to check whether strategy outputs are complete, specific, and grounded in business context.

## Example 1: B2B SaaS

Prompt:

```text
We are a US B2B SaaS company selling developer observability tools. We want to enter China and generate qualified leads. We have English blogs, docs, and YouTube demos, but no Chinese team yet.
```

Expected shape:

- Prioritize Zhihu, WeChat, Bilibili, and possibly CSDN or InfoQ China.
- Emphasize educational, technical, and search-oriented content.
- Recommend adapting blogs/docs into Chinese Q&A, guides, and demo videos.
- Flag need for Chinese technical copywriting and community response capability.
- Avoid over-prioritizing Xiaohongshu or Douyin unless there is a strong video/reach reason.

## Example 2: Consumer Lifestyle Brand

Prompt:

```text
We are an Australian skincare brand launching in China. We have Instagram and TikTok videos, product photography, influencer content, and a modest monthly budget.
```

Expected shape:

- Prioritize Xiaohongshu and Douyin, with WeChat for retention and owned communication.
- Emphasize brand, lifestyle, UGC-style, tutorial, and product proof content.
- Recommend adapting Instagram/TikTok assets, not direct reposting.
- Include account readiness around Chinese product claims, profile assets, and customer inquiry handling.

## Example 3: Manufacturing Exporter

Prompt:

```text
We manufacture industrial sensors in Germany and want Chinese distributors and enterprise buyers to discover us.
```

Expected shape:

- Prioritize Zhihu, WeChat, Baijiahao, and possibly Bilibili for demos.
- Recommend search-oriented and educational content: use cases, comparisons, industry guides, technical explainers.
- Focus less on viral consumer platforms.
- Include lead capture, sales handoff, and credibility-building assets.

## Quality Checklist

A good output:

- Analyzes the business before recommending platforms.
- Separates primary, secondary, and experimental platforms.
- Explains why each platform fits.
- Maps content formats to platform behavior.
- Includes localization, account readiness, and roadmap guidance.
- Names assumptions and uncertainty.
- Avoids definitive claims about current platform rules unless verified.
