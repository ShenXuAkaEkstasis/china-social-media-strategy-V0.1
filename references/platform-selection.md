# Platform Selection

Recommend Chinese social media platforms based on fit, not popularity. Consider business type, product traits, objective, content format, search behavior, user engagement, resource capacity, and operating capability.

## Selection Principles

- Match the platform to the buyer journey.
- Separate primary platforms from secondary or experimental platforms.
- Explain what each platform is expected to do for the business.
- Include niche vertical communities when they fit better than mainstream platforms.
- Avoid recommending every major platform by default.

## Common Platform Roles

| Platform | Best Fit | Typical Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| WeChat | Official communication, relationship management, private traffic, customer education | Owned channel and long-term trust |
| Zhihu | Search intent, professional answers, B2B expertise, complex products | Thought leadership and lead trust |
| Bilibili | Tutorials, product demos, long-form education, technical or enthusiast audiences | Education and product understanding |
| Xiaohongshu | Lifestyle, brand discovery, community trust, consumer word-of-mouth | B2C brand awareness and consideration |
| Douyin | Short video, mass awareness, entertainment-led discovery, quick product hooks | Reach and attention |
| Baijiahao | Search visibility, evergreen articles, passive inbound discovery | Search-oriented content distribution |
| CSDN | Developers, technical software, programming education | Developer credibility |
| InfoQ China | Enterprise technology, architecture, engineering leadership | Technical authority |
| 人人都是产品经理 | Product managers, SaaS, internet products, PM education | Product and SaaS thought leadership |

## B2B Guidance

Prioritize platforms where users search for professional information and credibility signals:

- Zhihu for expert answers, comparison content, and search-led discovery.
- WeChat for official presence, nurturing, private traffic, webinars, and sales enablement.
- Bilibili for tutorials, demos, technical education, and complex product explanation.
- Baijiahao for evergreen articles and search visibility.
- Vertical communities such as CSDN, InfoQ China, and 人人都是产品经理 when the audience is specialized.

## B2C Guidance

Prioritize platforms with consumer discovery, trust, and shareable content:

- Xiaohongshu for lifestyle positioning, reviews, community trust, and UGC-style content.
- Douyin for short-form reach, demonstrations, entertainment-led distribution, and campaign awareness.
- WeChat for retention, loyalty, community, support, and repeat communication.
- Bilibili when the product benefits from in-depth reviews, demos, or enthusiast education.

## Recommendation Logic

### Bilibili

Recommend when the product needs tutorials, demonstrations, education, long-form explanation, or enthusiast/developer trust.

### Zhihu

Recommend when search intent, professional credibility, question-led discovery, comparison content, or lead generation matter.

### Xiaohongshu

Recommend when lifestyle context, brand perception, peer trust, reviews, or community interaction are important.

### Douyin

Recommend when short-form video, mass awareness, visual hooks, entertainment, demos, or viral reach are realistic.

### WeChat

Recommend when the business needs an official channel, long-term relationship management, private communities, newsletters, webinars, support, or sales nurturing.

### Baijiahao

Recommend when evergreen content, passive inbound discovery, search visibility, and article syndication are useful.

## Platform Output

For every recommended platform, provide:

- Primary, secondary, or experimental role
- Why it is recommended
- What content should be published
- What business objective it supports
- Required resources and operating difficulty
- Early success indicators
