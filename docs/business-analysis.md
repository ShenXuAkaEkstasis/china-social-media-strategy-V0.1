# Business Analysis

## Purpose

Before recommending any China social media platform, the AI must first understand the business.

Never recommend platforms before completing the business analysis.

---

## Information to Collect

The AI should ask the following questions.

### 1. Company

- What does your company do?
- Which country are you based in?
- Are you already operating in China?

### 2. Business Type

- Is your business B2B or B2C?
- Who is your target customer?

### 3. Product

- What product or service are you promoting?
- Is it software, hardware, consumer goods, education, AI, manufacturing, or another industry?

### 4. Business Goal

What is your primary objective?

Examples:

- Brand awareness
- Lead generation
- Sales
- Community building
- Customer education
- Customer support

### 5. Product Characteristics

- Does your product require explanation?
- Does it require demonstrations?
- Does it require long-form content?
- Is trust important before purchase?
- Is branding important?
- Is search traffic important?

### 6. Resources

- Do you already have content?
- Do you have a marketing team?
- Do you have a Chinese-speaking team?
- What is your estimated budget?

---

## Output

After collecting the information, summarize:

- Business type
- Product characteristics
- Marketing objectives
- Available resources

Only then continue to Platform Selection.
