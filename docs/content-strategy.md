# Content Strategy

## Purpose

Recommend the most suitable content strategy based on the business, product characteristics, customer journey, and selected platforms.

Content strategy should always be determined before execution.

---

## General Principles

Content recommendations should consider:

- Product complexity
- Customer awareness
- Purchase decision process
- Business objectives
- Platform characteristics
- Available resources

Different products require different content strategies.

---

## Educational Content

Recommend educational content when:

- The product is difficult to understand.
- Users need product demonstrations.
- Customer education is required.
- The buying process is complex.

Typical content includes:

- Tutorials
- Product walkthroughs
- Feature demonstrations
- FAQs
- Case studies

---

## Brand Content

Recommend brand-focused content when:

- Brand awareness is a priority.
- Emotional connection influences purchasing.
- Lifestyle positioning is important.

Typical content includes:

- Brand stories
- User stories
- Lifestyle content
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Community interaction

---

## Search-Oriented Content

Recommend search-oriented content when:

- Users actively search for solutions.
- Long-term inbound traffic is important.
- Professional credibility matters.

Typical content includes:

- Articles
- Industry analysis
- Guides
- Comparison articles
- Knowledge sharing

---

## Entertainment Content

Recommend entertainment-focused content when:

- Viral exposure is important.
- Short-form content performs well.
- Community engagement is the primary objective.

Typical content includes:

- Memes
- Trending topics
- Short videos
- Interactive posts

---

## Content Localization

Do not simply translate overseas content.

Evaluate whether the content should be:

- Reused
- Adapted
- Completely recreated

Localization should consider:

- Chinese culture
- Platform behavior
- User expectations
- Current trends

---

## Initial Recommendation

For companies entering China for the first time, existing overseas content may be reused during the initial stage.

As the business develops, progressively increase localized content.

---

## Output

Recommend:

- Primary content strategy
- Secondary content strategy
- Suggested content formats
- Recommended publishing frequency
- Localization priorities
