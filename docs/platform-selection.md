# Platform Selection

## Purpose

Recommend the most suitable Chinese social media platforms based on the company's business type, product characteristics, marketing objectives, content strategy, and available resources.

Platform recommendations should never be based on popularity alone.

Every recommendation must include a clear explanation.

---

## General Principles

Platform selection should consider the following factors:

- Business type (B2B or B2C)
- Product characteristics
- Content format
- Marketing objectives
- Search behavior
- User engagement
- Available resources
- Operating capability

---

## B2B

For B2B businesses, prioritize platforms where users actively search for professional information and industry knowledge.

Typical recommendations include:

- Zhihu
- WeChat
- Bilibili
- Baijiahao

Additional industry communities may also be recommended depending on the industry.

Examples include:

- CSDN
- InfoQ China
- 人人都是产品经理
- Other vertical communities

---

## B2C

For B2C businesses, prioritize platforms with strong consumer engagement and brand influence.

Typical recommendations include:

- Xiaohongshu
- Douyin
- WeChat

Platform selection should still depend on the product and business objectives.

---

## Platform Recommendation Logic

The AI should explain why each platform is recommended.

Examples include:

### Bilibili

Recommend when:

- The product requires tutorials.
- The product requires demonstrations.
- Long-form educational content is important.
- The customer needs to understand the product before purchasing.

---

### Zhihu

Recommend when:

- Search intent is strong.
- Users actively seek professional answers.
- Lead generation is important.
- Trust and expertise influence purchasing decisions.

---

### Xiaohongshu

Recommend when:

- Branding is important.
- Lifestyle influences purchasing decisions.
- Community interaction is valuable.
- User-generated content is beneficial.

---

### Douyin

Recommend when:

- Short-form video is suitable.
- Brand exposure is the primary objective.
- Entertainment and viral distribution are important.

---

### WeChat

Recommend when:

- Long-term customer relationships are required.
- Community management is important.
- Official communication channels are needed.

---

### Baijiahao

Recommend when:

- Search visibility is important.
- Evergreen content is valuable.
- Passive inbound traffic is preferred.
- AI search and search engines are expected to discover the content.

---

## Additional Platform Discovery

When appropriate, recommend additional niche communities based on the user's industry.

Do not limit recommendations to the six major platforms.

The AI may recommend vertical communities that better match the target audience.

---

## Output

For every recommended platform, explain:

- Why it is recommended.
- What type of content should be published.
- What business objective it supports.
- Whether it is recommended as a primary or secondary platform.
